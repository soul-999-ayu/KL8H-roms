Important Disclaimer

Warning: Unlocking the bootloader is the first step to modifying your device, but it comes with risks. This process will completely wipe all your data (photos, apps, etc.) and may void your device's warranty. Proceed at your own risk.

You only need to do this once. If your bootloader is already unlocked, you can skip this guide.

Part 1: On-Device Preparation

Before you can run any commands, you must prepare your phone.

1. Enable Developer Options: Go to Settings > My phone > Version Info and tap the Build number repeatedly (about 7-10 times) until you see a message saying "You are now a developer!"

2. Add Tecno Account: Go to Settings > Log In to TECNO ID and sign in with your Tecno account. This is required for the OEM unlocking option to become available.

3. Enable Critical Toggles: Go to the new menu at Settings > System > Developer options and enable the following two options:

  • OEM unlocking: This allows the bootloader to be unlocked.
  • USB debugging: This allows your phone to receive commands from a computer or another phone.

Part 2: The Unlocking Process

You can run these commands from a PC (Windows/Linux) with ADB/Fastboot installed, or from another rooted Android phone using an app like Termux.

1. Connect your prepared Tecno phone to your PC or second phone.

2. Open a terminal (or Command Prompt/Termux).

3. First, reboot your device into fastboot mode by running:

adb reboot bootloader

4. Once your phone shows the fastboot screen, run the unlock command:

fastboot flashing unlock

Part 3: Confirming on Your Phone

After running the fastboot command, your phone's screen will change to a confirmation prompt.

FINAL WARNING: This is your last chance. Confirming this step will trigger an immediate factory reset and wipe all data on your phone.

1. Use the Volume Up key on your phone to confirm your choice.

Your phone will begin the unlocking and wiping process. It will then reboot automatically. The first boot will take longer than usual as it sets itself up from scratch. Congratulations, your bootloader is now unlocked!
